About Us

Beyond the Bend was formed only for competing in our school's Battle of the Bands. At first we didn't know how it worked and we were pretty much newbies at that time cause we just started our first year of high-school. Starting in 2013, we formed our band and we called it "Kids with their shoes on" It was kind of cheesy or so I thought and we lost. The following year we decided to call ourselves "Beyond the Bend" And we worked on composing our entry song and cover. Our original song was titled, "The Rise" and of course, I as the writer of the song, was very proud to call it a masterpiece. The competition went on and we got third place, and out of nowhere we started to become famous, more of an underdog story. 2015 arrived, new school year, new faces. Our band lost some it's original members, the bassist and drummer and we struggled to find replacements, but we luckily found them and they did great. There came our second original song titled, "Chain Drop". The song was a success as well but not as successful as "The Rise". We got third place again and again and again and again. We were in third place for 5 years in a row. We would change members every year, they come and go. In 2016, we merged with a band called "The Immortal Fridays" because we are both short on members and we made a promise to defeat our rival band called "Generation G". Generation G has been in first place for 4 years in a row and since our band and theirs are consistent in our places, we decided to call make a rival our of each other. 2017 was the last year of my co-founder, lead vocalist, Jared Cortado. I was forced to take his place and lead he band to victory but it was all the same. My compositions got rusty, music arranging got messy and we were unorganized. 2018 was my last year with the band, cause I had to move to an another country. Year 2019, the "new blood" members exceeded my expectations and they were able to maintain our position as the third best band and that was when only one original member was left to lead them, our lead guitarist, Adrian Panesa.
